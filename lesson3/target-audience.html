Target Audience

The target audience of the Meteo-con.com web site is described below:

Who: journalists, students, teachers, professors, academics, scientists, agricultural business, event business, builders, engineers, and much more.

Age: 12 to 100 years

Technology: Modern and provide the best forecast information. Usually it is a big require service for meteorological journalists

Income: income levels will typically be between $0 - $100,000 annually.

Motivation: improve knowledge of mainstream climate technologies and the best applications of Meteo-con in the enterprise, organization, or educational establishment.
