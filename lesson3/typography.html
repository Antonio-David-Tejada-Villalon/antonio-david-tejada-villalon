Typography

The primary text fonts are Roboto (sans-serif) is a free, modern, legible, and clear web font that is less eye strain and easier to read on screens. It's really good to use it on a blog or website, to improve visual style and usability. Alegreya (serif) is developed as a serif typeface, originally intended for literary texts. It achieves a dynamic and varied rhythm that is stimulating in the reading of long texts and is of great performance.

Site Header is in Roboto typeface with 50px, color: #1F213D, Sample
