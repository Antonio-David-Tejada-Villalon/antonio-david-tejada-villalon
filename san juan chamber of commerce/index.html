Purpose and Goals

Our entity is a non-profit civil organization, constituted on March 22, 2021, integrated by representatives of producing and exporting, agricultural, agribusiness, industrial, and tourism service companies. As active and persistent founding members, they are guided by the spirit of ensuring a better future for San Juan and support their budgets for all activities with their social dues.

SJCC is a non-profit organization whose purpose is:

  • To promote domestic trade and foreign trade to achieve its expansion;
  • To diversify domestic and foreign markets;
  • To provide its members with opportunities to increase trade and/or cultural relations with other inland provinces and other countries.
  • We defend the interests of producers and industrial exporters;
  • We ensure that we provide high-quality services at home and abroad in the province;
  • Finally, we signed an international agreement to ensure fair conditions and prices for exports from our province.
